Shola Akinlade

Co-Founder and CEO, Paystack

The Visionary

Shola Akinlade is a visionary Nigerian software engineer and entrepreneur, best known as the co-founder and CEO of Paystack. With a passion for technology and a commitment to innovation, Shola has played a pivotal role in revolutionizing the African fintech landscape. Under his leadership, Paystack has become one of the leading payment processing platforms in Africa, empowering businesses to accept payments online and fostering financial inclusion across the continent. Shola's dedication to creating seamless payment solutions has not only transformed the way businesses operate but has also contributed significantly to the growth of e-commerce in Africa.
